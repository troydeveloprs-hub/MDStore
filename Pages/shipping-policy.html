Shipping Policy

Shipping Overview

At MDBoutiquee, we strive to deliver your orders as quickly and safely as possible. We ship to all 50 US states and select international destinations.

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Domestic Shipping Rates

Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): 50 LE — Free on orders over 500 LE

Express Shipping (1-2 business days): 120 LE

Same Day Delivery: 200 LE

International Shipping

We currently ship to all governorates in Egypt. Shipping rates and times vary by location:

Cairo & Giza (1-2 business days): 40 LE

Alexandria & Delta (2-3 business days): 55 LE

Upper Egypt & Remote Areas (3-5 business days): 70 LE

Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your package by clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email or by visiting the carrier's website directly.

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost, damaged, or significantly delayed, please contact us at hello@mdboutiquee2.com and we will work with the carrier to resolve the issue. For damaged items, please include photos of the packaging and product in your email.

MDBoutiquee is not responsible for packages that are marked as delivered but not received. We recommend shipping to a secure location or using signature confirmation for high-value orders.

P.O. Boxes & APO/FPO

We can ship to P.O. boxes via USPS Standard Shipping only. Express and Overnight options are not available for P.O. Box addresses. APO/FPO addresses are supported via USPS Priority Mail.
